What to Pack

Q: What should I bring with me?
A: This ultimately depends on your destination. Each planet has a different climate, and dressing for Mercury versus Neptune will be very different. Around 2-3 months before your trip, you will receive a pamphlet about your destination that will give you additional details about the weather and activities of the planet of your choosing.
Q: Will I need a passport?
A: You will need to bring your passport and another government-issued ID before you are able to board the spacecraft. This is to help ensure the safety of you and other passengers.
Q: Can I bring my own space suit?
A: For the safety of both you and our neighbor planets only titanium grade company issued space-wear is allowed for an additional fee. Any non-company issued space-wear will be confiscated upon boarding, and you will not be allowed to leave the spacecraft.

Eating & Drinks

Q: How many restaurants are there aboard the spacecraft?
A: There are over two dozen restaurants from all over the world for you to choose from.
Q: If I have a dietary restriction, should I be concerned?
A: We ensure a positive experience for everybody, including those with dietary restrictions. Whether you’re vegan, vegetarian, or gluten free, you’ll be able to enjoy food from every single restaurant and concession stand on the ship.
Q: How many meals are included with my ticket?
A: All packages come with with food included. There will be dinner is the only meal that has a scheduled time. We do offer a breakfast buffet, and our dozens of restaurants will be open all day!

Getting to & from Port

Q: How long does it take to get to my destination?
A: It depends. If you choose to venture to a closer planet like Venus or Mercury, then it will take a shorter amount of time. If you’re going all the way to Neptune, it may take about a week longer. But don’t fret, because you’ll get to see some amazing sights along the way.
Q: Will there be public transport on alien planets?
A: We offer transport to and from the ship and various locations at each destination, and don’t worry, these are included in your ticket price.
Q: Do you offer ways to sleep through the trip?
A: Of course! We offer a wide array of options for those that wish to only experience their destinations, from cryopods to sleeping pills.

General Questions

Q: Are these vessels safe for travel?
A: Safety is our top priority here at Helios Space Expeditions. All of our spacecrafts are inspected monthly, as well as right before travelling to ensure the safety of all of our guests, staff, and crew.
Q: Is there medical assistance on board?

A: Yes, medical services are provided at no additional cost to you. Only licensed doctors and nurses work on our spacecrafts, and they will be sure to assist you with any medical issues you may have while traveling with us.

Q: What if I lose an item on another planet?
A: Just let our friendly staff know and we will try to locate your item as quickly as possible. Though keep in mind, each of our destinations are massive. We will try our best to find the item, but we might not be able to.
